A friendly coding conference for budding builders of websites,
apps and useful digital tools.

EXPLORE SESSIONS

Oh, come, oh ye learners!

Tall Conf brings curious students together for practical sessions, simple projects and conversations about learning technology one step at a time.

OPENING KEYNOTE

Start with a big idea.

Meet the conference host and hear how simple websites can solve real problems in schools, businesses and communities.

HTML AND CSS LAB

Build your first Layout.

Practice headings, buttons, images, spacing, colors and flexbox rows while building a real conference page.

TEAM BUILD

Turn a rough sketch into a page.

Work with a partner, choose a topic, draw the sections, and translate the design into clean HTML and CSS.

DESIGN REVIEW

Make it clear and readable.

Check font sizes, contrast, images, shapes, section spacing and whether the page still works on a smaller screen.

DEMO DAY

Show what you built.

Present your one-page site, explain your layout choices, and share one CSS skill you improved during the project.
